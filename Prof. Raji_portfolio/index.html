About Me

Pioneering research in autonomous systems and wireless technologies

Academic Profile

Dr. P. Rajalakshmi is a distinguished Professor and researcher specializing in Autonomous Navigation Technologies, Wireless Communications, and Cyber-Physical Systems. With over 15 years of academic and research experience, she has made significant contributions to the fields of autonomous systems, drone-based sensing, and wireless sensor networks.

Currently serving as a Professor at IIT Hyderabad, Dr. Rajalakshmi leads groundbreaking research initiatives in autonomous navigation testbeds and living labs. Her work focuses on developing next-generation technologies for aerial and terrestrial autonomous systems, with applications in smart cities, environmental monitoring, and industrial automation.

She has successfully supervised numerous PhD students and has been the principal investigator for several high-impact research projects funded by government agencies and industry partners. Her research has been published in top-tier international journals and conferences.
